Mandatory Fun

A chance for the team to bond using a combination of nature and fear. We will be spending half a day in the country, running around, getting muddy, and competing seriously against our fellow team members!

The plan.

We are going to spend the morning running the orienteering course over at Shotover County Park. The approximate start time is 9:30am and we expect to finish around 12:00pm where we will then find some food.

9:30am start-time, don't be late!

Teams will consist of 3 members and have already been decided, and will be revealed in the mornings briefing! Teams are final (no swaps)!

The course is approximately 4 kilometers, and should really take no more than 2 hours, dependant on your abilities. The course is quite hilly though, so be warned that it might be a little tough!

You will need.

  • Serious Footwear

    You are going to be walking/running around a muddy landscape, make sure you are prepared!

  • Extra Wardrobe

    Given you will be expected to go back to work afterwards, it might be an idea if you had a change of clothes to get in. The clothes you wear will get muddy.

  • Warm/Waterproof Clothes

    We will be outside for the whole morning, it is expected to be cold and it is expected to rain, so please make every effort to be warm (layers) and waterproof.

  • Water

    You will be so wet that the last think on your mind will be water, but hydration is key to an effective orienteerer!

The location.

Shotover Hill is located just on the other side of the ring-road from Headington. It is an easy walk from the top of Wood Farm (if you get off the number 4 bus at the aptly named Titup Hall Drive)

If you have any problems getting there please let the team know, getting back to the office afterwards is easier and will be sorted.

Orienteerwhaaaat?

The ancient art of taking a map and working out where things are using only the power of the Earths magnetic force! Orienteering consists of you finding locations on a map, which is on something called 'paper', and jotting down a secret code placed on a post at that location.

The quickest one back to the start wins, and neccesary deductions are made if you can't find a post!
